Set Your Goals!

Goal setting is an integral part of academic and personal development. Articulating your goals helps transform nebulous desires into actionable items. As you fill out this form, think carefully about what you wish to accomplish and how you plan to do it.


Choose a descriptive yet concise name for your goal. It should be something that instantly reminds you of what you want to achieve.


This field helps you articulate your motivation. Knowing why you are pursuing a goal can fuel your dedication to achieving it.


Setting a deadline can add a sense of urgency and focus. Make it realistic but challenging.


Think about the preparatory steps required to achieve your goal. Calculate the time needed for each and sum it up.
